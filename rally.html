What & Who

We will build teams of three to four people. Each team will run through the office and look for dirty spots on the walls. The spots are marked with a Post-its and each Post-it will indicate the amount of points your team earns for cleaning up that dirty spots. There might even be hidden levels like cleaning up the fridge. Who knows... The team with the most points will win the golden mop and basically rescue princess Peach Toadstool. Please feel free to create your team beforehand and (optional) show up in Super mario dress. Please RSVP and bring your mates along.
